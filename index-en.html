I am a first-year student in a Bachelor of Technology (BUT) in Networks and Telecommunications in Aubière, France.

I am 18 years old and I moved from Ukraine to study in France.

As part of my training at the IUT of Aubière, I am learning the fundamental concepts of networking, security, and telecommunications through lectures, tutorials, practical work, and supervised projects. This program allows me to acquire a solid foundation that I can apply and deepen in the professional world.

I would like to pursue my second year as an apprentice (work-study program) with a rhythm of 1 month at school and 1 month in a company.
